Hannah's Top 3 Movies

The Grand Budapest Hotel

This Wes Anderson masterpiece is my favourite film.

Visuals: the set design, costumes, bakery items, and everything in between has a beautiful artistic quality. So much so, that if a film was a painting in the Louvre, it'd be this film.

Plot: the plot is quite entertaining as well, beautifully written & acted!!

Legally Blonde

The perfect 'You Go Girl' film

Plot: the female empowerment theme is spot on

Cast: Reese Witherspoon is the perfect as a determined blond ready to conquer Harvard (what, like it's hard?!)

White Christmas

My favorite Christmas film.

Feeling: a nice cozy movie to watch to get in the mood of christmas

Cast: fantastic cast including Bing Crosby, Danny Kaye, and Rosemary Clooney
